About me

Outside the cursor, I've always been fascinated by how things work behind the scenes. What began as an interest in visual design evolved into a curiosity about how people think, make decisions, and interact with technology.

Today, that curiosity shapes my work as a Product Designer. Whether I'm designing scalable platforms, complex B2B workflows, or design systems, I enjoy finding clarity in complexity and creating solutions that feel intuitive for users while remaining scalable for teams.

At the heart of my process is empathy. I believe great products come from understanding the people, contexts, and challenges behind the requirements, then turning those insights into experiences that are intuitive, accessible, and built to last.
